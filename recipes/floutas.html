Recipe for Floutas

Here we have floutas en salsa verde that is a traditionl Mexican recipe that is well known in essentially all Mexican households. This dish is comprised of fried corn tortillas filled with shredded chicken breast and topped with salsa verde, sour cream, and queso fresco. This dish is very simple to execute and the ingredients are very easy to find! Try out this recipe and your family will be begging for more!

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Vegetable oil
  • Shredded chicken breast
  • Salsa verde
  • Sour cream
  • Queso fresco

Steps

  1. In a frying pan, add adequate vegetable oil and heat the oil on medium-high heat.
  2. Fill corn tortillas with shredded chicken breast and roll.
  3. Place the filled corn tortillas in the hot oil and fry them until light brown.
  4. Place floutas on a plate and top with salsa verde, sour cream, and queso fresco to you liking.
  5. Enjoy!
